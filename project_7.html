Task:
This excel file contains data from the international rating agency. Need to do a different experience of reading data rather than from the excel file.

Main data:
a. Country with ISO code and its rating.
b. 10 economic KPIs (key performance indicators) per country.
c. Each KPI has values for 10 years.

What does the user needs:
a. Can quickly see 10 KPIs of current (OR selected) year of a particular country.
b. Have a possibility to see trends over the years of the KPIs for one (or multiple) countries.
c. Compare KPI values of current (OR selected) year across countries from one dedicated credit rating group OR manually selected.
