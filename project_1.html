User:

Description:
A functional web application that acts as an auxiliary addition to a project management application such as redmine. Opens up quick access to employees with various parameters, with the ability to use them for a project. It also provides a complete summary and display of the necessary information on the Gantt chart.

Key features:
Logistics, effective control and quality distribution of employees' time in the project environment, identification of empty areas in the workflow. Suitable for large and small companies with a staff of 20 people or more.

Basic and Features:
Search by projects and by employees; sorting by skills, vacations, sick leave, birthdays, employment; filtering, categorization by profile departments, the ability to add and edit vacations, sick leaves, assign a person to a project; a Gantt chart displays a person's workload, projects, planned vacations and sick leaves. The necessary data is transferred from Redmine via api.
