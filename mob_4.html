Birbs (2021)

Project type:
Pet project

Description:
Birbs helps you keep in touch with family and friends. Birbs will not let you forget about scheduled appointments and upcoming events. Birbs will remind you that to give your loved one a birthday present, you will be ready to congratulate your friend on their wedding anniversary, and the christening of your cousin's child will not come as a surprise to you. With the help of an interactive magazine, you will be able to see a complete picture of your social activity. Birbs is about communication and strengthening social relations. Birbs is a web application that runs without installation and works equally well on any device. All notifications will come to your email address so you don't miss anything.

Platform:
Web App
